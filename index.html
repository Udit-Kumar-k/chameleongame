Tap to vote the imposter:

Round: 1

How to Play

  • Enter the number of players and imposters, and choose a keyword (or generate one).
  • Each player taps their name to see their word (or if they are the imposter).
  • After all have seen their word, the first player starts a discussion to find the imposter(s).
  • Players vote to kick out who they think is the imposter. If all imposters are caught, civilians win. If imposters equal or outnumber civilians, imposters win.
  • Use the checkboxes for extra options, like showing the category or revealing imposters when kicked.

Note: The imposter does not know the keyword and must blend in!
